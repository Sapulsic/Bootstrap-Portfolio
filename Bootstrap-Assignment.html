About Me



Born and raised in Dallas. Lived here all my life. Grew up loving to play video game, such as Halo, Runescape, Call of Duty. As a kid I wanted to become a Game Designer due to playing and loving all these games and their stories they told. I never got to that point of trying to become one, but ended up finding another type of programming I also enjoyed and that was Web Developing.
